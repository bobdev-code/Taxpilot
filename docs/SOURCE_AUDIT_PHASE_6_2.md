# Phase 6.2 Source Audit

Purpose: record the source base for the German rule register without turning TaxPilot into legal advice.

Primary official source locators:

- de-estg-4: https://www.gesetze-im-internet.de/estg/__4.html
- de-estg-6: https://www.gesetze-im-internet.de/estg/__6.html
- de-ustg-14: https://www.gesetze-im-internet.de/ustg_1980/__14.html
- de-ustdv-33: https://www.gesetze-im-internet.de/ustdv_1980/__33.html

Product interpretation:

- EStG 4: business purpose, sensitive categories, restricted expense review.
- EStG 6: equipment and asset treatment review.
- UStG 14: invoice field completeness and electronic invoice awareness.
- UStDV 33: small-amount invoice path.
- AO 147: retention and evidence availability.
- BMF GoBD: electronic record traceability and auditability reminders.

Safety boundary:

- ask for evidence;
- flag accountant review;
- record source IDs;
- never claim final deductibility;
- never claim VAT deduction certainty;
- never claim GoBD certification.
